SERVICES

We offer varieties of quality and first class services to customers.This makes us stand out from others.These services provided are enumerated below;


General Printing

With the use of modern printers especially the EPSON printers, customers are able to get their documents ranging from small to large to be printed. Customers also get the chance to select range of colours to be used on their documents. They don't have to wait to get their documents printed since the printers are fast.

Lamination

Prevent your vital documents from mess through our modern laminators. Special layer of rubber is used to cover documents and are inserted into the machines' rolllers to apply heat and pressure to harden the rubber. The laminated documents are able to last for longer period of time.

Scanning

Turn your hard copy documents to soft copy through our powerful and modern scanners. In this digital world of online registration, certain vital documents may be required to be submitted in a soft copy form and scanners are the right device to complete the tasks. These devices are capable of converting your hard copy document to a compatible format of soft copy which can be saved and viewed on your PC, mobile phones, tablets, among others.

Online Registration

Customers who opt to register online for certain institutions such as GES,DV Lottery,Colleges,Universities,among others, are warmly welcomed. We have a high speed network which ensures successful uploading of customers' data.

Internet Cafe

We also run internet cafe which enables customers to access the internet at an affordable price.The high speed internet allows faster reload of multiple pages on the internet and also prevent connection breakages especially when using webcams.

Graphic Design

With the emerging graphic design tools such as Adobe Photoshop,Adobe Illustrator,Adobe Premier,and many more,our skilled workers are able to create excellent graphic designs according to the specifications of our customers.Designs such as wedding cards,funeral cards and banners,flyers,3D animations,and many more, are offered by our professional workers.

Passport Pictures

Customers who are required to upload their passport picture in an online registration process are warmly welcome.
Moreover,customers who want passport piture for personal reasons are also welcome.Pictures are taken by powerful and high definition digital camera, therefore pictures taken are very enhanced.

Photocopy

Customers can get their documents duplicated as many times they want to help reduce the stress as a result of misplacing certain documents.
